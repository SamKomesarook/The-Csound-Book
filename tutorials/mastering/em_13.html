Csound for Dummies
Hopefully you've made it this far and you're starting to "get it." If this is the case, then you've also come to the realization that Csound is some seriously heady stuff. I am sure you are wondering if you have to intellectualize everything first, or can you work intuitively with Csound? Suprisingly, the answer is YES!


If you're the type of sound designer who starts with a good preset and transforms it in unique, personal and exotic ways, then I have good news for you. Csound is for tweakers too!
