Paris Smaragdis was born in 1973 in Athens, Greece. In 1992 he joined IRMA,
Athens where he received training and performed research on audio analysis and
synthesis algorithms. In 1995 he received with honors his B.Mus. in electronic
music, with a concentration in sound design, from Berklee College of Music,
Boston, USA, where he studied with Prof. Boulanger. In 1997 he graduated with an
M.Sc. in media technology from MIT. He is currently pursuing a doctoral degree
at MIT wehile working as a research assistant in the field of machine audition at
the MIT Media Laboratory with Prof. Vercoe. His current interest is forming
mathematical models of low-level auditory perception.
