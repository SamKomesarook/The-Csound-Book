I am currently employed as Senior Music Technician at Bath Spa University
College.  My main interest is in computer music, particularly the use of Csound. 
When I was asked to contribute a piece, I wanted to produce a work that had
something in common with a track from my lastest CD  "BEATSYSTEM 2297 ", released
September 1997 on EM:T Records. The result is a piece that uses the same source
material and similar processes to the similarly named track on the CD. I am
particularly interested in the possibilities of granular synthesis - I like the
fluid textures it can produce, and the variety of ways in which it can be
implemented.
