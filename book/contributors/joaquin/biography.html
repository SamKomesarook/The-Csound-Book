Jacob Joaquin (b.1976) graduated from Berklee College of Music in 1998 with
a degree in Music Synthesis. After graduation, Jacob served as musical
assistant to Richard Boulanger. In 1999, he received the Max V. Mathews
Award from the Berklee Music Synthesis Department. He performed in the 1999
Boston Cyberarts Festival. Jacob now resides in Mountain View California
with his newly wed Cathlyn.
