Dave Phillips is a musician, teacher, writer, and lecturer based in Ohio. His
formal studies include composition study with Michael Jon Fink and guitar
instruction with Jerry Willard and Sophocles Papas. He has been working with
music software on microcomputers since 1985, and he has been working exclusively
with the Linux operating system since 1996. His publications include numerous
articles in the Linux Journal, and he is currently working on a book about music
and sound software for Linux.
