Noel Bush

http://www.erols.com/noelbush
noelbush@erols.com

Born in Louisville, Kentucky, 1974. Undergraduate study at Bard College, New
York. Current residence in Brooklyn, New York. Composer in tape and computer
media, writer. Recent works for multitrack tape include: Abschere (1996), In the
Invisible World (1996), Insulation (1996), ECCEPT (1995), Landing On Vanishing
Everything (1994), Good Morning (1994), GONE (1993), Glory-Be Acrostic (1993),
Knotted Voices: an ongoing series of radically recast Bob Dylan songs (1992-
present). Recent works for computer: tract / intract (1995±present), Wall-to-wall
Baby (1994-5), Did you hear a deer? (1993).
