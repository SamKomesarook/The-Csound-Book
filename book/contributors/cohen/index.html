Jean-Luc Cohen

tb808@yahoo.com

Jean-Luc Cohen started his career in music as an organist in Paris. Very early on
he became interested in the use of the computer as a musical tool and later on
studied music synthesis at the Berklee College of music in Boston. There he
focused on minimalism, serialism and computer music. It is also there that he was
first exposed to Csound by Dr. Richard Boulanger. He is still am active performer
on the Boston scene with several industrial bands and pursues a career in
multimedia. The primary focus of his work is in software synthesis and
composition as well as its integration with other medias, such as video and
computer graphics.
