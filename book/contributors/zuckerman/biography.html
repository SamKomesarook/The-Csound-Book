Born and raised in the tiny town of Slate Hill, New York, Jeremy Zuckerman began
his musical career as a guitarist with little interest in or knowledge of
electronic music. It wasn’t until midway through his education at the Berklee
College of Music in Boston that he was exposed to computer music--he hasn’t been
the same since. Jeremy received his Bachelor of Music from the Berklee College of
Music and is currently working towards an MFA in Composition--New Media at the
California Institute of the Arts in Valencia. Jeremy is also co-founder of
Dynamic Impulse, a progressive mastering facility.
