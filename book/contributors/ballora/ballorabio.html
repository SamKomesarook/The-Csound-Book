Mark Ballora was raised in California and received a BA in Theatre Arts
from UCLA in 1980. He then moved to New York City and became involved in
composing music and sound scores for modern dance, experimental films, and
radio dramas. He did his master's work at NYU, receiving an MM in Music
Technology in 1995 and an MA in Music Composition in 1996. He is currently
a Ph.D candidate in the Music Technology at McGill University in Montreal,
where he is researching auditory display and the audification of data sets.
