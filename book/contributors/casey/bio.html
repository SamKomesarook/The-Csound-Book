Michael Casey earned a Ph.D. from the Massachusetts Institute of
Technology Media Laboratory in 1998. He is currently employed as a
research scientist at MERL, in Cambridge Massachusetts, where he is
conducting research in structured audio authoring and extraction tools for
music and sound effects. Michael is also an active participant in the
International Standards Organization (ISO) for which he is a co-chair of
the MPEG-7 audio task group.
