David's education includes a Bachelor of Music (Organ), West Texas State
University, Canyon, 1973; and two years graduate study (Organ and Music History &
Literature), Baylor University, Waco, Texas. David's career in the recording and
audio post production industries began part-time in 1971, then full time
beginning in 1975. He has recorded and/or mixed hundreds of albums, and hundreds,
if not thousands of jingles, and has mixed, either as production or post mixer
many television programs, including many live-to-air concerts. David has received
three Emmy nominations, and platinum and gold albums. He resides in Dallas, Texas
with his wife and son, and a wide variety of small pets. In addition to his real
job, he is currently serving as Interim Organist at First United Lutheran Church
in Dallas.
