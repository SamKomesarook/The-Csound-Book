Steve Antosca has a degree in Music from Tulane University and a Master’s
degree in Computer Music Composition from The Peabody Conservatory of
Johns Hopkins University. Currently he teaches Computer Music and
Composition at The Levine School of Music in Washington, DC where he is
the Chair of the Music Technology Department. He is a founder and
Artistic Director of the Music Technology Department’s concert series
crossCurrent, dedicated to performing innovative new music and presenting
artists from related fields. He recently received a grant from the US
Department of Education, Fund for Improvement of Post Secondary Education
(FIPSE) to develop a three year program to create a curriculum for and to
teach music educators how to use music technology in the classroom.
