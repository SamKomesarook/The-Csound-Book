Allan Schindler is Professor of Composition and Director of the Computer Music
Center at Eastman School of Music. His compositions, evenly divided between
purely acoustic works and those employing computer- generated sounds, are
available in a series of commercial compact disk recordings and in published
editions by semar editore (Europe) and World Wide Music (US).
