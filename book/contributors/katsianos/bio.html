Themis Katsianos

tkatsia@music.mcgill.ca

Themis Katsianos was born in Montreal, Canada in 1965. He studied Physics at the
National University of Athens, and High-Energy Physics at McGill University,
Canada. He also studied Music at Concordia University, Canada. Themis is
currently completing a Master's degree in Computer Applications in Music, at
McGill University, under the supervision of Bruce Pennycook. His interests
include, among others, Computer Music and Digital Signal Processing, Quantum
Mechanics, jazz drumming, and chess.
