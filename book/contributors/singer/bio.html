Eric Singer, cybernetic engineer/electronic musician/fringe artist:
constructor of electronic devices, fabricator of interactive software,
player of instruments, executor of hazardous performances. Mr. Singer
holds a B.S in Computer Engineering (Carnegie Mellon University), a diploma
in Music Synthesis (Berklee College of Music) and an M.S. in Computer
Science (New York University). He performs on saxophones, alternative
controllers and various electronic devices of his own design. He is
enthusiastic about intermingling electronic music, computer graphics,
controllers, robotics and pyrotechnics to innovative effect.
