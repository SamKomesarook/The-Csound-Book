Tolve


tlv@tuna.net

Tolve is the producer/composer of The Dante Project: Inferno, a multimedia
theatrical play slated to open Off Broadway in the fall of 2000. He has
worked under grants from NYSCA, SICA, MPTF, and FFTF. In 1990 he co-founded
the Staten Island Center for the Performing Arts (Sicpa) serving as the
group's managing director, resident composer and on one occasion,
writer/director. He also collaborated with Carol Kalil and Andrea Tabacchi
on the performative theatrical/film event Phrenology Illustrated at La Mama
ETC.

On clarinet, saxophone, and synthesizer, he has appeared at such venues as
Carnegie Hall, the Felt Forum, Lincoln Center and CBGB. He is a member of
the Screen Actors' Guild and has credits in theater, film and television.
Tolve formerly served as General Manager of Tish & Snooky's NYC, Inc.
(Manic Panic Haircolor & Cosmetics), holds a BM from the Crane School of
Music, and has taught in the New York City High Schools.
