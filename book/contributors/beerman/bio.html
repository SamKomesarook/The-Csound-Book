Burton Beerman

bbeerma@opie.bgsu.edu

Burton Beerman is Director of the MidAmerican Center for Contemporary Music At
Bowling Green State University. Recent performances of Burton Beerman’s works
have taken place at such venues as the Holland Fest and Paradiso in Amsterdam,
the ORF RadioFunkhaus in Vienna, New York’s CAMI Hall, Modern Museum of Art and
the Walter Reade Gallery in Lincoln Center, Chopin Hall in Mexico City, Town Hall
in Brussels, the American Cultural Centre and the Cite Universitaire Theatre in
Paris, Japan, Australia, Budapest, Canada, and New Zealand. Burton was in
residence at STEIM Research Center in Amsterdam, where he integrated his wireless
multiple channel dance system, the BodySynth and STEIM’s Image/ine and LiSa
allowing real-time control of music and video and text with a dancer’s movement.
CNN- CNNI (Turner Broadcasting) FUTUREWATCH, THE WORLD TODAY and HEADLINE NEWS
aired a feature story on the technology in his works. Presently, he is music
director for the Hungarian-based dance company, Jula Berger Dance Ensemble
(formerly of the National Ballet Company of Hungary) and will be touring Eastern
and Western Europe and the United States with this ensemble during 2000 and 2001.
