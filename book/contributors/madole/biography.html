Dave Madole

madole@mills.edu

Dave Madole has been Technical Director of the Center for Contemporary Music at
Mills College in Oakland, California since 1993. Before that he was the
Coordinator of Computer Applications at the Center for Music Research at FSU in
Tallahassee, Forida. He did both his undergraduate and graduate work in
Composition at Princeton University (BA '79, MFA '86) where he studied with Paul
Lansky, J.K. Randall and Peter Westergaard. He began composing for electronic
media in 1977. He has been programming since 1981 and since 1983 he has been
involved in developing much software for musical use in various programming
languages for various machines, but remains a composer at heart, and continues to
compose. He also plays the fiddle, a little, and likes to read Finnegans Wake.
