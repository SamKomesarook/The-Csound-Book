Martin Dupras

mup015@bangor.ac.uk

Martin Dupras is currently pursuing a Ph.D. in Electroacoustic
Music at the University of Wales, Bangor (Wales.) He holds a
M.A. in Electroacoustic Music from Dartmouth College, and a
B.Mus. in Electroacoustic Music from Universit de Montral.
His compositions have been played in the U.S.A., Canada,
Brazil, and U.K.
