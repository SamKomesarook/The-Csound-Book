Kyle Gann, Village Voice, wrote "By a slim margin, Harrington is the most
intriguing composer I've found on the web..." Born in Mississippi, raised
in New Orleans, Jeff Harrington's funk-influenced classical music has been
played all over the world. He currently supports himself as lead
programmer for HyperTV Networks, an interactive TV company, and
distributes his music as Acrobat scores and MP3's on the Internet.
