Erez Webman

webman@piano.technion.ac.il

Born in 1968, Israel. I am a musician who works in his own recording studio.
During the last five years, I have been coordinating the production of four CD
projects for "Chains" record label. My involvement in these projects included
composing, arranging, playing and sound engineering. In addition, I have been
involved in various recording and mixing projects in different styles of music. I
have experienced teaching of sound and recording techniques to students of
diverse backgrounds. For the last two years, my main interest has been the
development of algorithms of signal processing for musical applications. One of
my works "The Musical Effect of Cross-Vocoding" was published in the last ICMC
proceedings. Another paper regarding a new method for envelope detection is now
in preparation. I am now concluding my studies for B.Sc. in computer-science at
the Technion- Israel Institute of Technology. I have chosen to combine these
studies with my concurrent musical activities. Thus, building a tuition program
that supports and strengthens technical abilities that are relevant for my work.
My experience in software engineering includes design and development of
commercial systems, such as a large-scale communications software package for the
English software company "Aldoda Ltd.".
