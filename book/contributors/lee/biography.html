Allan Lee graduated with a BSc. in Physics and Electronics from The University of
Ulster in 1988. Following this he carried out research work in parallel
computing using Transputers, Distributed Array Processor (DAP) and Parallel
Virtual Machine (PVM) for The Northern Ireland Transputer Support Centre and The
Parallel Computer Centre at The Queen’s University of Belfast. In 1992 he was
appointed Senior Computer Engineer in charge of systems and network management
for the Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering at The Queen’s
University of Belfast. Since 1993, he has also been working on a part-time
postgraduate research programme at The Queen’s University of Belfast in
algorithmic sound synthesis techniques mainly using Csound real-time WIN32 code.
Allan is currently Senior Systems Administrator in charge of the IT
infrastructure for APiON Ltd. in Belfast, Northern Ireland.
