Sound Example

This simple example was obtained with the MORPHER.ORC and MORPHER.SCO instrument. It uses only two patterns (to keep it fast), an 808 bass drum, and an acoustic open hi-hat. It is stimulated with pink noise.

The sample files used were:

soundin.10 - 808 bass drum (2.782 sec)

soundin.11 - open hi-hat (2.247 sec)

soundin.99 - pink noise (2.0 sec)

This is merely for demonstration purposes. I've had useful and unusual results when combining drum loops. Although you cant get rid of the noisy nature of this instrument, I strongly advise for instance time-stretching two different loops to the same length, and merging them together. Using something like noise to stimulate it, you can (with some luck) get positive results. There are interesting things that can be done with synth sounds, drum sounds, sound effects and even acoustic samples, as well. The more disparate the samples are, the fatter chance you have of finding something rare.
