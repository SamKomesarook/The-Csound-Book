IV.4. The score - Section 5

This is the tape part under the first Neruda’s poem, “La sangre quemante” (The burning blood). It uses just instrument 2 (filtered pulses) and instrument 4 (flou). One of the main problems, in writing for voice (or instruments) and tape, is the notation. I chose to write the soprano part in conventional music notations (i.e., divided in measures, without too many difficult tempos: mainly 4/4, 5/4 and 3/4), and to notate the tape parte on three staves, also in conventional notation, warning the soprano that the tape part is not exactly as notated. But all worked well. If you would like to see the soprano part, contact me.
