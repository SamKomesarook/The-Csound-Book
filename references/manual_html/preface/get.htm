Obtaining Csound

Where to Get Public Csound and the Csound Manual

Public Csound is available for download from : 

   ftp://ftp.maths.bath.ac.uk/pub/dream

or 

   ftp://ftp.musique.umontreal.ca/pub/mirrors/dream

This Hypertext Edition of the manual, as well as the Print Edition, in Adobe Acrobat format (.pdf) are available for browser download from: 

   http://www.lakewoodsound.com/csound

or via anonymous ftp from:

   ftp://ftp.csounds.com/manual

Sound files referenced in the manual may be obtained from:

   ftp://ftp.maths.bath.ac.uk/pub/dream/documentation/sounds/modelling/
