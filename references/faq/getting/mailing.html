How to Join the Csound Mailing List:
To subscribe: Send an e-mail to csound-subscribe@lists.bath.ac.uk
To specify God@heaven.af.mil as your subscription address: Send mail to:

<csound-subscribe God=heaven.af.mil@lists.bath.ac.uk>

You'll get a confirmation message to that address.When you receive that message, simply reply to complete your subscription.
To unsubscribe: Send email to csound-unsubscribe@lists.bath.ac.uk

 

How to Join the cSounds.com Mailing List:
You can join the Csounds.com Mailing List directly from www.csounds.com.

 

How to Join the CECILIA Discussion List:
To subscribe to the CECILIA discussion list, send a message to: majordomo@ginette.musique.umontreal.ca
In the body of the message write the following message:

subscribe cecilia
To send message to the list, address the messages to: cecilia@ginette.musique.umontreal.ca
Messages sent to the list are broadcast to all members of the list.

 

How to Join Csound-Unix-Dev
Send an e-mail to Majordomo@ilogic.com.au
In the first line of the message, submit: subscribe csound-unix-dev

 

How to join the Algo-comp Mailing List
Send an email to majordom@heinous.isca.uiowa.edu
In the first line of the message, submit: algo-comp

 

How to join the Composers Desktop Project Mailing List
Send an email to mailbase@mailbase.ac.uk
In the first line of the message, submit: join composers-desktop-project
